Oonagh

Senta-Sofia Delliponti (born April 16, 1990 in Wolfsburg ) is a German singer, songwriter and actress. Since January 2014 she is under the stage name Oonagh (German pronunciation: [ˈuːnäːɣ]). Her signature musical style is inspired by the mystical lore of J. R. R. Tolkien's universe and by ethnic sounds throughout the world.

She had her breakthrough in Star Search, a television talent show. She released a Christmas album with TV Allstars in 2003. In 2015, she was a guest singer on the Celtic Woman album Celtic Woman: Destiny.[3] She sang the theme song to Big Brother in Germany in 2007.
